Introduction

This web-site allows you to write a story using AI.

You supply the background to the story, the plot and the characters and the AI will develop a story based on this information.

Prompts and Responses

You provide prompts that the AI will respond to. The AI will use previous prompts and responses to generate the next response in the story.

The AI will not be perfect. It will often misinterpret what you want to say or forget something that happened a few messages ago so you can edit the latest response and make corrections.

If the AI gets it totally wrong, you can edit a previous prompt. This will delete all prompts and responses after the prompt you edited.

Adding a Story

AI Guidance are instructions for the AI to use throughout the story. You can change them but it won’t affect posts you’ve already created. Changing them radically will likely result in later parts of the story reading differently to earlier parts.

AI Guidance can be populated with a template using the Get button which will bring up a drop-down list. Select one and tailor it for your needs afterwards.

Once you press Post, the Update Story screen will be displayed.

Updating and Printing

You can use the Stories link to come back to a story and edit it or add further prompts

You can also generate a Word document (docx) using the print option. This will be downloaded to the default location on your device (Download on a PC). It will not include the user prompts but will include the characters.
